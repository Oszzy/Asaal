About Lavender Essential Oil

Our lavender essential oil is extracted through steam distillation from the flowering tops of Lavandula angustifolia, grown in the pristine fields of Provence, France. This variety, known as true lavender, produces the finest quality oil with the most therapeutic benefits.

The oil has a fresh, floral, and slightly sweet aroma that's instantly recognizable and widely loved. Its versatility makes it one of the most popular essential oils in aromatherapy, suitable for both beginners and experienced users.

How to Use Lavender Oil

Aromatherapy

Add 3-5 drops to your diffuser with water. Perfect for creating a calming atmosphere in bedrooms, living rooms, or offices.

Topical Application

Always dilute with a carrier oil before applying to skin. Use 2-3 drops per tablespoon of carrier oil (such as jojoba or coconut oil).

Bath

Add 5-10 drops to a warm bath for a relaxing soak. Mix with Epsom salts first for better dispersion.

Sleep Support

Place 2-3 drops on a tissue and place near your pillow, or add to a pillow spray with distilled water.

Massage

Create a massage blend by adding 5-7 drops to 30ml of carrier oil. Perfect for relaxation and muscle tension relief.
