Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days. We offer free standard shipping on orders over £50.

Are your essential oils organic?

Yes! All our essential oils are certified organic by the Soil Association. We never use pesticides, herbicides, or synthetic fertilizers in our sourcing.

How do I use essential oils safely?

Always dilute essential oils before topical use. We recommend 2-3% dilution for adults (10-15 drops per tablespoon of carrier oil). Never ingest essential oils unless under professional guidance.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, return it for a full refund or exchange.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for businesses and practitioners. Please contact us at wholesale@asaal.co.uk for more information.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also track your order through your account dashboard on our website.
