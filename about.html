Our Journey

From a small passion project to a trusted wellness brand, discover how ASSAL came to be.

The Beginning

ASSAL was born from a personal journey of healing. Our founder, Sarah Chen, discovered the transformative power of essential oils during her battle with chronic stress and anxiety. Frustrated by synthetic solutions that masked symptoms rather than addressing root causes, she turned to ancient wisdom and natural remedies.

What started as personal experimentation in her kitchen grew into a passion for sharing these healing gifts with others. Sarah spent years studying aromatherapy, traveling to source locations, and building relationships with sustainable farmers and distillers around the world.

In 2018, ASSAL officially launched with just three carefully crafted blends. Today, we offer over 20 pure essential oils and custom blends, each one carrying the same intention: to support healing, promote wellness, and honor the sacred relationship between humans and plants.

Milestones

The Discovery

2016

Sarah's personal healing journey with essential oils begins. First experiments with lavender and eucalyptus create profound shifts in stress levels and sleep quality.

First Formulations

2017

Initial blends created for friends and family. The "Stress Relief" and "Deep Sleep" formulations receive overwhelmingly positive feedback and requests for more.

ASSAL is Born

2018

Official company launch with three signature blends. The name "ASSAL" means "pure honey" in Arabic, symbolizing the sweet essence of nature's healing gifts.

Global Partnerships

2019-2020

Direct relationships established with organic farms in France, Bulgaria, and Australia. Commitment to sustainable and ethical sourcing becomes core to our mission.

Certification & Growth

2021-2022

Achieved organic certification and expanded to 20+ pure essential oils. Custom blend creator launched, allowing customers to craft personalized wellness solutions.

Community Impact

2023-2024

Launched wellness education programs and sustainable packaging initiatives. Serving over 50,000 customers worldwide while maintaining our commitment to quality and purity.

Certifications & Quality

Our commitment to quality is validated by industry-leading certifications and rigorous testing standards.

USDA Organic

All our essential oils are certified organic, ensuring they're free from pesticides, synthetic fertilizers, and harmful chemicals.

Cruelty-Free

We never test on animals. All our products are certified cruelty-free by Leaping Bunny, the gold standard in cruelty-free certification.

Fair Trade

We support fair trade practices, ensuring our farming partners receive fair compensation and work in safe, sustainable conditions.
