Our Story

Founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make pure, organic essential oils accessible to everyone. We believe in the power of nature to enhance wellbeing and transform daily routines into moments of mindfulness.

Our Mission

At Asaal, we're passionate about connecting people with the healing power of plants. Our mission is to provide the highest quality essential oils while promoting sustainable practices and ethical sourcing.

We believe that wellness should be accessible to all, which is why we're committed to offering premium products at fair prices, backed by education and exceptional customer service.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Purity First

We never compromise on quality. Every batch of essential oil is rigorously tested to ensure it meets our high standards for purity and potency.

Our Journey

From a small idea to a trusted wellness brand

The Beginning

2020

Asaal was founded with a vision to make premium essential oils accessible to everyone. We started with just three single oils sourced from trusted organic farms.

First Milestone

2021

Expanded our product line to include expertly crafted blends and starter kits. Reached 1,000 happy customers and established our quality testing laboratory.

Sustainability Focus

2022

Launched our sustainability initiative, introducing eco-friendly packaging and carbon-neutral shipping. Partnered with fair trade farming cooperatives.

Community Growth

2023

Built a thriving community of wellness enthusiasts. Launched educational resources and workshops to help customers get the most from their essential oils.

Looking Forward

2024+

Continuing to innovate and expand our product range while maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer education.
